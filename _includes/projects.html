Notable Projects




"MiLO" - Mind-Controlled Locomotive

  • EEG, OpenBCI, Twilio, Python, Javascript, Android; see our paper for more details

MiLO is a fully functioning brain-controlled wheelchair primarily aimed for individuals with paralysis from the neck-down using realtime EEG data from an OpenBCI to control movement. It includes safety measures such as wall tracking & auto-stopping with obstacle sensors. The project also includes a location tracking & stress detection app for caretakers to monitor wheelchair using patients. Our project was featured on multiple news channels, including CBC & CTV, and we published a paper about it in the 2019 IEEE International Conference on Systems, Man & Cybernetics!

UpcycleAI

  • Python, Gazebo, Microsoft Azure

UpcycleAI is our national-winning robotic arm that uses AI & computer vision to re-structure how companies recycle fabrics in the textile industry, which has one of the largest contributors to the global carbon footprint and heavily impacts air and water pollution.

"mySamy"

  • Python, Watson API, Javascript, HTML/CSS

MySamy is our hackathon-winning interactive help app for children that can detect stress & mental illness, & shares relevant resources for its users. Our project won first place in the IVEY business model challenge, and 2nd place in the IBM challenge.
